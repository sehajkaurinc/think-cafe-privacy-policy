Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Important: This privacy policy explains how Think Cafe collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our cafe management and POS application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and basic profile information
  • Business Information: Cafe name, contact details, and business settings you provide
  • User Preferences: App settings, theme preferences, and configuration choices

1.2 Business Data

  • Product Information: Menu items, prices, categories, and product images
  • Sales Data: Transaction records, order history, and billing information
  • Customer Data: Table management information and order details
  • Inventory Data: Stock levels, product quantities, and supplier information

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Usage Analytics: App performance data, feature usage, and error reports
  • Network Information: IP address and connection status for sync operations

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Process and manage cafe orders and transactions
  • Maintain product catalogs and inventory
  • Generate reports and analytics for your business
  • Provide table management and billing services

2.2 Data Synchronization

  • Sync your data across devices using Firebase
  • Backup your business data to Google Drive (optional)
  • Ensure data consistency and availability

2.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google Services

  • Google Sign-In: For secure authentication and account management
  • Firebase: For data storage, synchronization, and real-time updates
  • Google Drive: For optional data backup and restore functionality
  • Google Mobile Ads: For displaying advertisements (with user consent)

3.2 Barcode Services

  • OpenFoodFacts API: For product information lookup via barcode scanning
  • Local Database: For storing and retrieving product information

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

  • Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • All local data is encrypted and protected
  • Data remains on your device unless you choose to sync

4.2 Cloud Storage

  • Data synced to Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Google Drive backups are encrypted and secure
  • We use industry-standard security practices

4.3 Data Access

  • Only you have access to your business data
  • We do not share your data with third parties without consent
  • Data access is logged and monitored for security

5. Permissions and Device Access

5.1 Camera Permission

  • Used for barcode scanning to identify products
  • Camera access is only active during scanning
  • No images are stored or transmitted

5.2 Storage Permission

  • Used for saving receipts, reports, and app data
  • Allows importing/exporting business data
  • Required for local database operations

5.3 Bluetooth Permission

  • Used for connecting to thermal printers
  • Enables receipt printing functionality
  • Only connects to authorized devices

5.4 Location Permission

  • Used for Bluetooth device discovery
  • Required for thermal printer connectivity
  • Location data is not collected or stored

6. Advertising and Analytics

6.1 Google Mobile Ads

  • We display advertisements to support app development
  • Ads are shown only in designated areas of the app
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Ad interactions are tracked for performance measurement

6.2 App Analytics

  • We collect anonymous usage statistics
  • Data helps us improve app performance and features
  • No personal or business data is included in analytics

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Data Retention

  • Your business data is retained as long as you use the app
  • Backup data is kept for the duration of your subscription
  • Analytics data is retained for up to 2 years

7.2 Data Deletion

  • You can delete your account and all associated data
  • Local data can be cleared by uninstalling the app
  • Cloud data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Control

  • View and download your business data at any time
  • Modify or delete your account information
  • Control data synchronization preferences
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

8.2 Privacy Settings

  • Disable data synchronization if desired
  • Control advertising preferences
  • Manage backup and restore settings
  • Configure app permissions

9. Children's Privacy

Think Cafe is designed for business use and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your data receives adequate protection.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or via email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Compliance and Legal

12.1 Data Protection Laws

  • We comply with applicable data protection regulations
  • GDPR compliance for European users
  • CCPA compliance for California residents
  • Other applicable local privacy laws

12.2 Data Processing Legal Basis

  • Contract performance for core app functionality
  • Legitimate interest for app improvement
  • Consent for optional features and advertising
  • Legal obligation for record keeping
